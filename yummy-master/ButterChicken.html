Butter Chicken

By Sri - India

  • Recipe:
    • Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces. Marinate chicken in chilli, ginger paste, garlic, and salt.
    • Chop tomatoes into bite size pieces. Chop onion.
    • Fry the chicken for 8 minutes until cooked through and set aside. We are now ready to create the sauce.
    • Fry the onions in butter until translucent.
    • Then add tomatoes, and fry for another couple minutes until the mixture softens.
    • Add cashews, water, sugar, salt, chilli powder, garam masala, and garlic. Continue to saut√© for 15 minutes until the mixture becomes stewy.
    • Blend the mixture with a hand blender until it turns into a smooth paste.
    • Pass the sauce through a sieve to catch any large pieces, and then mix the chicken in with the smooth sauce.
    • The butter chicken is ready to serve when warm.

    Time to Cook:

    30 mins

  • Ingredients:
    • 3 chicken breasts
    • 1 tbs chilli powder
    • 1 tsp ginger paste
    • 1 tsp garlic
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 4 tomatoes
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 tbs oil for frying
    • 1 tbs butter
    • 1/4 cup cashews
    • 5 tbs water
    • 1 tbs sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1 clove crushed garlic
    • 1 tsp garam masala
